Private windows

Winger can list and interact with private windows.

Private windows are currently not accessible to Winger. As far as Winger is concerned they do not exist.

To change access to private windows, go to the Details tab at the top this page and choose an option for Run in Private Windows.

Keyboard shortcut

The keyboard shortcut to open the pop-up panel is [current-shortcut]. (Default: [default-shortcut])

It can be changed in Firefox's extension shortcuts manager: Click the ⚙️ button above this page, click Manage Extension Shortcuts, and look for the Winger shortcut.
